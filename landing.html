Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Xyloclime Pro

What data sources do you use?

We use multiple tier-based data sources: Tier 1: NOAA GHCN stations (100% accuracy, 4,940 global stations for US/Canada). Tier 2: Visual Crossing (80-100% accuracy, global coverage). Tier 3: ECMWF IFS (~50% accuracy, fallback). Tier 4: ERA5 (~4% accuracy, last resort). The system automatically selects the best available source for each location.

How accurate is the snowfall data?

For US and Canadian projects, we provide 100% accuracy through direct NOAA station measurements from 4,940 weather stations. This eliminates the 96% error rate found in ERA5 reanalysis models used by generic weather apps. For other regions, we use Visual Crossing (80-100% accuracy) or ECMWF IFS.

What locations do you cover?

Global coverage across 127 countries. Our NOAA network includes 4,663 stations in the US, 277 in Canada, plus international stations worldwide. For locations without nearby NOAA stations, we seamlessly fall back to Visual Crossing or ECMWF global coverage.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties or fees. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period. We also offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use Firebase's enterprise-grade security with encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL) and storage. Your projects are private, backed up automatically, and only accessible to you. We never share your data with third parties.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through our secure Stripe payment processor. All payments are encrypted and PCI-compliant.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact us within 14 days of your purchase for a full refund—no questions asked.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, so you keep your current benefits until then.

What construction types does this work for?

Xyloclime Pro works for all outdoor construction: concrete pours, excavation, grading, roofing, asphalt paving, steel erection, masonry, and more. Our thresholds are based on industry-standard weather impact guidelines and can be customized for specific work types.

How far back does historical data go?

Free accounts get 30 days of history. Pro and Enterprise accounts get 10+ years of historical weather data, perfect for analyzing seasonal patterns and long-term climate trends.

Can I export my analysis?

Pro and Enterprise plans include full export capabilities. Generate professional PDF reports with charts and summaries, or export raw data to Excel for further analysis and integration with your existing workflows.

Do you offer team/multi-user plans?

Yes! Our Enterprise plan includes team collaboration features. Multiple users can share projects, collaborate on analysis, and maintain consistent weather assumptions across your organization. Contact us for custom team pricing.
